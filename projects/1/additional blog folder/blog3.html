First Client
June, 2016

The summer towards junior year seemed a bit too slow for my liking. I have been trying to follow tutorials on many websites without actually developing any programs. With the approach I took, you could say I wasted my time since I never practiced. Was my summer going to consist of tutorials but no practice? I feared that idea. To ease my mind a bit I decided to contact a friend of mine from high school. Whooh, I could say sending the messsage to him was a great decision at the time. Through our talk, I was told that he wanted to develop an app in the near future. Was invited to jump in the wagon and gave the ok immedietely! From there on, I met my first client and developed my first app which was soon to be published later that year. -Ahmad
